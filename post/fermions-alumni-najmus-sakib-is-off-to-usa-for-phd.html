POST Fermion's alumni Najmus Sakib is off to USA for Ph.D. 12 January 2022 | Asif Iqbal

Congratulations to Md. Najmus Sakib (Physics Discipline, 16 batch, KU) for getting enrolled in the Physics Ph.D. program at the Department of Physics and Astronomy, George Mason University, Fairfax, Virginia, USA. He graduated from Physics Discipline, Khulna University, in 2020. He was a former instructor for the ‘Fermion Physics Club’.

Full profile:

  • Md. Najmus Sakib
  • Physics Discipline
  • Batch: 16
  • Honor’s CGPA: 3.65/4.00
  • IELTS: 7.0 (L-8.0, R-6.0, W-6.5, S-6.5)
  • GRE: 306(Q-161, V-145, AW-3.0)
  • Scholarship type: Full funded, Complete tuition remission
  • Program: Physics Ph.D. program
  • University Name: George Mason University, Fairfax, Virginia, USA

Work Experience:

  • Instructor of Community of Physics: Khulna Section (Taught Course: Prelude Natural Philosophy (PNP) in Community of Physics: Khulna Section in 2019).
  • Volunteering at Bangladesh Physics Olympiad and Bangladesh Science Society.
  • Former Instructor of Fermion Physics Club, Khulna University.
  • Tutoring and mentoring high school students at Higher Secondary Physics and Chemistry.

No Publications.

Project Experience:

  • Survey on the working principle of the instruments of Bangladesh Meteorological Department (from 25 January 2020 – 27 January 2020).
  • Survey on the working principle of Cox’s Bazaar Radar Station (from 28 January 2020 to 30 January 2020).

Fermion Physics Club wishes to congratulate him on his outstanding achievement. We believe that the future holds even more promise for him, with this scholarship serving as the cornerstone for his future success.
