OUR Objectives
ACADEMIC AID Our curriculum is based on academic syllabus. We plan to conduct rigorous study involving problem solving and lab experimenting.
UPHOLDING PHYSICS We want to change the view of people about physics being a pure subject. We want to uphold this awesome subject.
BUILDING PORTFOLIO Member's profesional work life will be previewed in this website. This website will serve as portfolio for the members.
SKILLS DEVELOPMENT We focus on developing required skills for a physicist. Such as Microsoft Office, MATLAB, Programming etc.
