WELCOME TO FERMION PHYSICS CLUB
Our Vision

Our vision is to create an inclination of our students towards Physics and help them become passionate about it. We aim to help students to transform themselves into contributing members to Physics. We help them to develop a solid understanding of the fundamental principles of physics, an ability to work with the concepts mathematically, and a functional understanding of how these ideas play out in the real world. Students will develop a flexible and creative problem-solving ability through participating in our club activities which have been designed specially to fulfill these purposes.
