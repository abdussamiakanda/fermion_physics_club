Standardized tests provide a baseline against which college authorities may evaluate all candidates equally, and they can aid admissions officers in deciding amongst a group of outstanding candidates. The tests provide several advantages and provide colleges with information about how candidates compare to their peers. According to Kaplan Test Prep, a high GRE score is one of the most important aspects that helps students stand out from other applicants when applying for scholarships and assistantships. As a result, a high test score is not just a means to a student's top preferred school but also a method to fund it.
