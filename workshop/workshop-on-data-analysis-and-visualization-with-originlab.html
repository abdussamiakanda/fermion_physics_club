WORKSHOP Workshop on Data Analysis and Visualization with OriginLab 6 August 2022

OriginLab is a data analysis and graphing software of choice for over half a million scientists and engineers in commercial industries, academia, and government laboratories worldwide. OriginLab offers an easy-to-use interface for beginners, combined with the ability to perform advanced customization as you become more familiar with the application. OriginLab graphs and analysis results can automatically update on data or parameter change, allowing you to create templates for repetitive tasks or to perform batch operations from the user interface, without the need for programming.

Take your data analysis skill to the next level with this workshop.
