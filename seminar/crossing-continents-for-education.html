SEMINAR Crossing Continents for Education December 2023

Join us for an inspiring talk by Md Abdus Sami Akanda, a Bangladeshi scholar who navigated cultural differences and academic challenges to pursue graduate education in the United States. In this candid session, Md Abdus Sami Akanda will share their personal story of crossing continents, from their early aspirations in Bangladesh to navigating the application process, adapting to a new academic environment, and ultimately thriving in U.S. graduate programs.  

The webinar is based on “Zoom” and the seminar will be recorded and uploaded to Youtube. There is a Q&A session. So joining the seminar gives attendees the opportunity to ask questions directly to the speakers.

Subscribing as an attendee means you'll continue to get emails of our sessions regularly. If you want us to remove you from the mailing list, please send us an email or reply to emails that were sent to you.

Speeches:

Title: Crossing Continents for Education: A Bangladeshi’s Pursuit of U.S. Graduate Degrees
Speaker:
Md Abdus Sami Akanda
