SEMINAR Physics Talk : Season 01 March 2021

Physics Talk 2021 is a webinar initiative from Fermion Physics Club. Coinciding with its goal, which is to make physics fun and commutable, Fermion Physics Club attempts its first webinar where the speakers talk about their works, career, and journey to pursue physics.

The webinar is based on “Zoom” and the seminar is recorded and uploaded to Youtube. There is a Q&A session. So joining the seminar gives attendees the opportunity to ask questions directly to the speakers.

Subscribing as an attendee means you’ll continue to get emails of our sessions regularly. If you want us to remove you from the mailing list, please send us an email or reply to emails that were sent to you. Adding the Google Calendar will allow you to stay updated with our schedules and be notified about the sessions.
