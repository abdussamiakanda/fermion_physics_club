SEMINAR Preparation For Standard Tests June 2023

'Preparation for Standard Tests' is a series of webinar, arranged by Fermion Physics Club. 'Standard Tests' referring IELTS, TOEFL, GRE and Physics GRE which is required for pursuing higher studies in abroad, this online seminar aims to provide actualities about the procedures, preparation, obstacles of the tests; as well as, several tips and strategies to make a better approach. This platform allows the participants to make direct communication with the one who have successfully accomplished this phase.  

The webinar is based on “Zoom” and the seminar will be recorded and uploaded to Youtube. There is a Q&A session. So joining the seminar gives attendees the opportunity to ask questions directly to the speakers.

Subscribing as an attendee means you'll continue to get emails of our sessions regularly. If you want us to remove you from the mailing list, please send us an email or reply to emails that were sent to you.
